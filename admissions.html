Eligibility Criteria

Undergraduate Programs

  • 10+2 or equivalent examination
  • Minimum 45-50% aggregate marks
  • Subject-specific requirements as per program
  • Valid identity and address proof

Postgraduate Programs

  • Bachelor's degree in relevant field
  • Minimum 50-55% aggregate marks
  • Entrance exam scores (if applicable)
  • Work experience (for some programs)

Required Documents

  • 10th & 12th mark sheets
  • Graduation mark sheets (for PG)
  • Transfer certificate
  • Migration certificate
  • Character certificate
  • Passport size photographs
  • Aadhaar card / Passport
